STEP 01

Name the People in Your Life 列出您生命中的重要人物

To begin, write down the names of the following 12 people. Although a person may fit more than one category, you need to compile a list of 12 different people. If there is no one who fits a category, name someone who is similar to the category description. For example, if you do not have a brother, select someone who is like a brother to you.

首先，請在以下12個類別中各寫下一個人的名字。雖然某人可能同時符合多個類別，但您必須列出12位不同的人。若某一類別沒有符合的人，請選一位與該描述相近的人。例如，若您沒有兄弟，請選一位像兄弟一樣的人。

STEP 02

Discover Your Constructs 發現您的個人建構

Next, take three of these people at a time, as indicated by the numbers in the following list. Then describe in what important way two of them are alike but different from the third. Put a word or phrase describing the two similar people in the Construct column and a description of the remaining person in the Contrast column.

接下來，按照以下編號，每次選取三位人物。描述其中兩位在某個重要方面相似，但與第三位不同之處。將描述那兩位相似者的詞語或短語填入建構欄，將描述第三位的詞語填入對比欄。

RESULT

Your Personal Construct Summary 您的個人建構摘要

This abbreviated version of Kelly's Rep Test provides a quick idea of the constructs you use to organize information about the people you know and meet. You may want to compare your responses with those of other test takers. No doubt you will find a few overlapping constructs, but also many you hadn't thought of. Of course, these differences in personal constructs represent differences in personality that should translate into individual differences in your behavior.

這是凱利Rep測驗的簡化版，能快速呈現您用來組織所認識之人的個人建構。您可以將您的回答與其他測試者比較。您會發現一些重疊的建構，但也會有許多您未曾想到的。這些個人建構的差異，代表了人格的差異，並會反映在您的行為上。
